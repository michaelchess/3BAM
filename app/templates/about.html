About:

The idea for Three Billion and Me came over the summer while I was working in the computational genetics lab of Dr. Mark Daly. I was talking to one of the researchers there, Brett, about my upcoming independent study project when he mentioned that there was a gap in the market for online tools allowing whole genome scan analysis. This seemed like an awesome thing to do so upon starting school in September I got to work. The project in its current form provides a straightforward way to understand the information contained in a VCF file. In just a few quick and easy steps you can check if you have any high risk of disease variants, any disease-implicated variants, and you can see all of your variants laid out in an easy to read table.

A special thanks to Brett, Doug, Menachem, Shaun, and Mom for giving me pointers and other help.
