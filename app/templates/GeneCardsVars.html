Other Health Variants:

The variants displayed on this page are those found in your genome that were included in the GeneCards list of genes related to disease. It is also important to note that although some of the variants included in this list do have a causal relationship to disease many do not and some even have a preventative role. This list is included less as an indication of impending medical problems and more as a way to explore the disease genes possibly related to yourself and your family. It should not be considered with the same importance as the variants reported on the Clinically Relevant Variants page.
