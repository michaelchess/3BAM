Welcome to 3 Billion and Me!

Have you ever found yourself stuck with a whole genome scan and no one to interpret it?
Do the scientists give unintelligible answers, and the people at 23 and Me just don't know enough about genetics?

Well, look no further, 3 Billion and Me will take you up on that challenge!
Our brand new website combines the most up to date genetic analysis tools, like PLINK and XBrowse with an easy to use and understandable interface to give you the best genome-sequence-interpretation experience possible.
